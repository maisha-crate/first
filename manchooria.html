ingredients for Veg manchooria

  • 1 Cup Grated Carrot
  • 1 Cup Grated Cabbage
  • 1 Cup Chopped Spring Onion
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped French Beans
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Capsicum
  • 1 Tablespoon Chopped Ginger
  • 1 Tablespoon Chopped Garlic
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Chopped Green Chillies
  • 2 Tablespoon Corn Flour
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Red Chilli Paste
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ajnimoto
  • Salt to Taste
  • Oil for Frying

Ingredients for Manchurian Sauce/Gravy:

  • 3 Tablespoon Oil
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Stock
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Spring Onion White
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Chopped Ginger
  • >1/2 Tablespoon Chopped Garlic
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Red Chilli Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Corn Flour

Procedure For manchooria:

In a wide bowl mix all the chopped and grated veggies. Add salt and also all other ingredients listed under
'Ingredients for Dumplings", except oil. Mix them well, do not add any water.
This will turn into a moist dough. Make small lemon size balls with the dough.
Heat oil in a kadai, slowly drop each dumpling into the hot oil.
Fry them in low medium flame till they turn into light golden brown.
Keep them on a kitchen napkin aside.
We can store these dumplings for further use also, kids like them very much.

Procedure For Dumplings:

Heat oil in another kadai, add chopped ginger and garlic. Fry them for a while.
Add chopped spring onion whites, fry them till they turn soft.
Add soy sauce, tomato sauce and red chilli sauce. Give a nice saute.
Then add water or vegetable stock.
Check salt and add it if necessary.
Add the above fried veg dumplings, allow them to boil in the sauce for 2 minutes.
Add water to corn flour and mix well to avoid lumps.
Add this slurry to the above sauce to get it thicken.
Transfer it into a serving bowl, temper it with some chopped spring onion greens.
Serve hot, spicy veg manchurian with Hakka Noodles or Veg Fried Rice.
